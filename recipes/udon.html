Udon

Description

This comforting Udon recipe is perfect for any time of the year.

Ingredients

  • Udon Noodles
  • Spinach
  • Shiitake Mushrooms
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Boil Noodles.
  2. Remove noodles from pot and add to dry one, add spinach and cook until limp.
  3. Mix in seasonings and mushrooms.
